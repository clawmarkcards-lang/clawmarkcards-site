About Claw Mark Cards

Claw Mark Cards was built out of the same energy that keeps you ripping — the rush of the hit, the sweat of the sweat, and the stories that come from the chase.

We're not a faceless storefront. We're collectors, competitors, and grinders who love the hobby and treat it like the ecosystem it really is.

The Mission

Turn every rip into something that leaves a mark — on your PC, on your bankroll, or on the experiences you have with other collectors.

  • Respect the money people put into the hobby.
  • Keep the product real, the breaks fair, and the energy high.
  • Reward loyalty and consistency with real perks and real value.

The People Behind The Mark

Claw Mark Cards is built on competition, brotherhood, and that constant itch to outwork, out-think, and out-hustle. It's not corporate. It's not sanitized. It's real-life grinders pushing for something bigger than just flipping wax.
