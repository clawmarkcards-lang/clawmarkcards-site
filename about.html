Claw Mark Cards was born from competition, brotherhood, and a relentless passion for sports and collecting.

We’re Hunter Lawrence (Hlaw) and Nick Speck (Speck) — best friends since grade school, raised on football and fueled by rivalry. Nick is a Chicago Bears lifer with Brian Urlacher as his all-time favorite, while Hunter rides with the Carolina Panthers and Cam Newton at the top of his list.

Forged in Competition

Our bond was built through Madden — thousands of head-to-head battles, tournament trips, and Top-100 Xbox Live rankings. When we walked in, the championship usually came down to us. It wasn’t just the wins — it was the grind, the respect, the fire.

Different Paths, Same Destination

Nick grew up in Reading, PA before landing in Myrtle Beach, SC. Hunter was born and raised in Myrtle. Two journeys — one vision.

Betting on Ourselves

We did the corporate climb. We learned a lot — but it built someone else’s dream. So we bet on ourselves. After years of vision, hustle, and planning, Claw Mark Cards is here. We’re not just breaking boxes — we’re building a movement.
